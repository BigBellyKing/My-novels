Chapter 265: System Fully Upgraded (Fifth Update, Requesting Subscriptions and Monthly Passes)

In the Wildspire Waste's Diablos's Den.

Accompanied by a mournful wail, the Diablos collapsed and died.

Song Ren couldn't help but burst into laughter: "Awesome!! Just a puny Diablos! A mere weakling!"

Looking at the Diablos's corpse on the ground, Song Ren couldn't resist taking a photo and posting it on his Moments.

Two minutes later, Zhang Yi sent a message: "Holy crap, Old Song, why did you switch to Gunlance? Weren't you determined to be a Longsword Azure Star?"

"What Long Sword? I don't understand what you're talking about? Besides, I'm doing this for the team. Didn't you guys say Long Swords easily cause friendly fire? So I switched, didn't I?" Song Ren engaged in a wave of selective amnesia.

Although the Long Sword was indeed cool, facing the rampage of the Diablos, he still chose to abandon his conviction.

It wasn't just Song Ren; many other players who were stuck on levels did the same.

They all adopted these "skipping class" methods, thoroughly enjoying the pleasure of bypassing challenges.

And after these players defeated the Diablos and Rathalos using these "skipping class" methods,

they didn't feel bored. On the contrary, they were filled with self-confidence.

They couldn't wait to challenge even stronger monsters later on.

Even some of the original comments re-emerged.

Monster Hunter: World's difficulty is underperforming!

The Gunlance and Light Bowgun aren't just skipping class; they're dropping out of school entirely!

Facing these inflated players, Chen Xu chuckled.

"Healed the scar, forgotten the pain. I hope you can still smile when you face BOSSes like Deviljho, Nergigante, Kirin, and Lunastra."

While players happily roamed and hunted in the New World, Chen Xu also opened his system, something he hadn't done in a long time.

He hadn't drawn a lottery in ages, and this time, Chen Xu was genuinely excited.

After all, since Minecraft launched, his points hadn't moved.

The points generated by Minecraft's success must be enormous.

With a hint of excitement, Chen Xu opened the system.

Units, tens, hundreds, thousands... nine digits, converted into lottery draws.

Goodness, he had accumulated over two thousand draws.

He had never been this wealthy!

Chen Xu was now very familiar with the matter of drawing lots.

He started drawing directly, beginning with 500 pulls.

Chen Xu was too lazy to even look at the golden flashes. After 500 pulls were done, he swept his gaze across the results.

There were no golden items, but he did spot a purple item.

'Keyboard Milk Spout Card (Self-only): After use, allows you to perform incredible actions in the game, accompanied by a commentator's predictions, bringing a large amount of positive attention to the game. (Sometimes milk spurting from the keyboard isn't necessarily a bad thing.)'

What the heck?!

Keyboard Milk Spout? Is this letting others "poison milk" him (jinx him)?

And the effect doesn't seem very powerful!

Bringing a large amount of positive attention? He wasn't short on money for promotion now.

Chen Xu glanced at it cursorily and then tossed it aside.

After that, he saw an item that caught his attention.

'Self-Service Shop Key *1: Can open the shop to purchase specified items.'

Able to open a shop to actively purchase items?

Could those golden AI visual enhancement technology cards he saw before be directly purchased then?

Looking at the key item in front of him, Chen Xu noticed a preview button next to it.

Trying to click it, a large pile of items popped up in front of Chen Xu.

However, after scanning through them from beginning to end, Chen Xu felt a little disappointed.

Because there were no items of gold quality or higher. Items like the 'Fear Converter' he obtained while making Outlast, and the 'Keyboard Milk Spout Card' he just got, were quite numerous, all purple quality. However, their prices were uniformly steep, starting with eight-digit points.

Clearly, this stuff was definitely overpriced. After all, the Fear Converter only earned a few million points in total, but it was being sold for eight digits?

Even unscrupulous merchants aren't this outrageous!

Besides those, there were also Memory Capsules and common skill trees, but each was priced at six digits.

"Too dark!" Chen Xu couldn't help but exclaim.

Then Chen Xu turned his attention to the other skill books.

Some new ones appeared, but most were those he had drawn before.

Item Design, Plot: 83 books.

Camera Work, Directing Expertise, Psychological Expertise: 120 books.

Architecture, Art Concept Art, Action Special Effects: 171 books.

Music, Sound Effects: 79 books.

Aside from those, the rest were all Memory Capsules.

"Consume a batch first, then save the remaining points for a while."

As Chen Xu used the skill books, he also pondered.

But the next moment, a prompt appeared on the screen: "Current skill value meets promotion conditions, enabling Stage Two."

It upgrades?!

Chen Xu froze for a moment, seeing the system in front of him burst into colorful light, revealing a new UI interface.

The Gashapon machine itself wasn't much different from before.

But why did the points for drawing increase to 1,000,000 per pull?

'Entering Stage Two, previously acquired points remain valid.'

'Stage Two: Corresponding points obtained, in addition to Emotion Points, will be comprehensively calculated based on Industry Influence.'

What does this mean?!

Looking at the brand new system, Chen Xu was stunned for a long time, only understanding the situation after a bit of research.

First, points still originated from players' emotions, just like before.

But the difference was the addition of an upper limit threshold: Industry Influence.

And what was called Industry Influence referred to the positive changes brought to the gaming industry.

For example, new game genres, and changes to the industry's existing rules.

Of course, they had to be positive. A classic negative example was Atari ET in his previous life, which led to the Video Game Crash.

At the same time, the item shop also entered Stage Two.

Looking at his points, he attempted a five-pull.

Chen Xu found that the items he drew were also different.

Directing Expertise *1

Psychological Expertise *2

World Ancient Civilizations (Ancient Greece) *1

World Ancient Civilizations (Norse Era) *1

It was no longer limited to just art and music; it was a comprehensive improvement.

But this influence!

If only he had known, he would have drawn earlier!

He was a step too late!

The establishment of the Nebula Games Platform was definitely a decision that garnered a huge amount of influence.

After all, the era of "channels are king" in the gaming industry had been directly shattered.

Although the Channel Alliance, along with TengHua and NetDragon, were still biding their time to counterattack, Chen Xu honestly wasn't afraid at all.

But now?

All gone!

Of course, it was still unknown if it was limited to game-related influence, but even if it was, Monster Hunter could still earn a good amount of influence.

And games like the previous Undertale, among others, had all brought significant Industry Influence.

Even Mirror (Game) was similar, profoundly changing the previous situation of "old lecherous men's adult games" having low quality.

Making adult games bigger and stronger, creating new glory ‚Äì that too was a kind of Industry Influence!

However, despite all this, the system's update gave Chen Xu a new plan for the future development of the Nebula Games Platform.

Isn't it all about bringing positive influence to the industry?!

He would start with one game to test the waters.

Looking at the brand new system interface, Chen Xu thought about it and decided to temporarily save his points.

After all, with the system upgrade, although the items had improved,

inflation had set in. His once wealthy life suddenly became meager.

Furthermore, with Chen Xu's current abilities, they were temporarily sufficient.

It wouldn't be too late to draw again when there was a need. And the key he obtained, he checked, could still be used, and the items in the shop had also changed to Stage Two system items.

Of course, the prices made his heart tremble a bit.

"I'll save up a bit more."

(End of Chapter)
