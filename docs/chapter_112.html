Days passed one by one, and Nebula Games began its promotional warm-up for Minecraft.

While players and industry designers were discussing this wave of sandbox trend.

Chen Xu's official Weibo post instantly drew the attention of countless players and industry designers.

Chen Xu's official Weibo post was very simple, containing only three words: New Game.

Following this, a video link was released.

However, upon clicking it, players who had been full of anticipation and curiosity were instantly stunned.

Because it was simply too unexpected for them.
