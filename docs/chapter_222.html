Chapter 221: Absolutely Mind-Boggling! (Sixth update, requesting subscriptions, monthly passes)

After two consecutive official Weibo account videos, many players had already guessed that this was Nebula Games' special promotional strategy for Minecraft.

Players who had previously built houses in the game, spurred on by the 'Along the River During the Qingming Festival' and 'Forbidden City' videos, were even more restless, unable to focus on food or drink.

Their minds churned with all sorts of operations, wishing they could immediately dive into the game world and experience it.

Although many players knew in their hearts that achieving projects on the scale of 'Along the River During the Qingming Festival' and 'Forbidden City' might be a bit of a stretch for them alone.

But surely some slightly simpler projects would be within their grasp, right?

In that instant, many players who had previously built matchbox houses had completely forgotten their past experiences.

Their minds were filled with images of magnificent structures.

It was like playing Dark Souls; even though they had just been thrashed by Gundyr the Ash in the game, seeing other veteran players defeat the Nameless King without taking damage suddenly made them feel capable again.

They felt as if they had learned a lot, thinking that while they might not be able to defeat the Nameless King, going back to fight Gundyr the Ash should be no problem at all.

This was the psychological activity of most players at this very moment.

As for the many designers in China, they too were marveling at this wave of promotion for Minecraft.

Clearly, the recent popularity had also shocked them.

Namely, a video was released every three days.

Calculating the time, it seemed like this pace was intended to continue updating until the game's official release date?

Because this third video was completely different from the previous 'Along the River During the Qingming Festival' and 'Forbidden City.'

Seeing the video title, many players were taken aback.

Wasn't it about building houses, building ancient cities?

Previously, whether it was the Forbidden City or Along the River During the Qingming Festival, many players felt shocked.

But they could also mentally visualize and imagine that those structures were built by themselves.

But what about this video now?

All the players were dumbfounded.

Inside the video was a massive piano constructed from blocks, complete with 88 black and white keys, a piano case, pedals, a soundboard, strings, and a hammer action mechanism.

While its appearance was impressive, it wasn't as astonishing as the previous "Along the River During the Qingming Festival" and "Forbidden City" videos.

But the most crucial point was that this thing moved!

And it genuinely produced sound.

As 'To Liva' began to play, the black and white keys constantly moved. Simultaneously, the camera slowly shifted to the piano's interior, showing the piano's dampers continually jumping and the hammer action mechanism beneath the strings striking them.
