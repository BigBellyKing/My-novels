Chapter 305: Negotiations Break Down, Prepare for Assault! (Requesting Subscriptions, Monthly Passes)

Under the setting sun, in a cemetery filled with gravestones, a middle-aged man rose from an unmarked grave.

'I never expected it. Everything happened too fast. A gunman, a single bullet, straight to the head.'

He stood up and walked forward.

'This one, I planted a Thermite charge, and a life was just extinguished.'

'It was I... who made a mistake only a rookie would make.'

The camera zoomed in, and only then did players realize that the photo on the tombstone was actually of the man himself.

'They hid a trap by the window. Remember to double-check before entering!'

The camera zoomed in again; the person on the tombstone was still him.

'Make good use of your ropes. Don't think you're a superman. Falling from the second floor... sometimes it's just that sudden!'

'Foolish, still foolish... always foolish.'

The middle-aged man took a deep breath and continued to walk forward.

All the gravestones along the way bore his photo.

At this moment, players watching the video understood.

Clearly, this wasn't some convoluted story about reincarnation.

Instead, it symbolized the man's actions as a player within the game.

'A gunman was ambushed there. By the time I noticed him... it was too late!'

'Knifed from behind, vertical explosive... This one, I ran out of the building and stepped directly on a Claymore. Being bold is good, but don't be greedy.'

'I remember every cause of my death, every foolish mistake I made, and every wrong judgment of the situation!'

'But I guarantee, it won't be me who falls in battle today!'

As his words fell, the camera zoomed in on the man's face, and the screen immediately went dark.

Only his narration remained.

'Are you ready? To initiate a meticulously planned assault.'

The next moment, the screen switched to the interior of a bank. In the center of the lobby, a group of hostages had their hands bound and mouths gagged, their faces filled with terror. Simultaneously, on the first floor, a group of terrorist personnel were planting bombs in an office.

Nearby, countless white-masked terrorist personnel were using roll-down wooden boards to seal off various critical passages.

Some white-masked terrorist personnel stood guard at various key points, shields in hand.

Meanwhile, the camera moved outside.

Elite special forces personnel from various countries stood ready for battle ‚Äì the Rainbow Team.

"Yes, it's them again."

"We can't let the bomb detonate!"

"But they refused to negotiate!"

"The safety of all hostages can only be entrusted to you!"

A middle-aged officer looked at the fully armed Rainbow Team, took a deep breath, closed his eyes, then opened them and said in a firm tone: "Now..."

"Negotiations break down, prepare for assault!"

As his voice fell.

The assault officially began.

All squad members had been ready for battle in their respective areas.

In the distance, Glaz was already in position, his sniper rifle set up, calmly locking down all possible terrorist locations.

Meanwhile, operators like Ying, Blitz, Montagne, Thermite, Ash, and Pulse split into two groups and assembled into position.

One group prepared to attack from the first floor, while the other ascended to the bank's rooftop via ropes, ready to launch a coordinated assault with the first floor team.

At this point, the screen switched back to the initial location. Two unmanned reconnaissance drones slipped through the gap in the door into the bank, transmitting internal information.

A notification sound came through all squad members' voice comms.

"One terrorist in front of the bank's main entrance counter, two terrorists to the left of the entrance, hostages have been moved to the second floor."

Facing the tightly shut bank main door, everyone exchanged glances. The next moment, Thermite stepped forward.

Then, the scene shifted indoors.

On the steel bank door, a crimson spark appeared, moving in a highly regular pattern.

It was as if someone were cutting open the door with a cutting torch, only much faster.

Before the white-masked terrorist personnel could figure it out, an astonishingly loud bang erupted the next moment.

'Boom!'

A puff of black smoke rose. The next moment, fragments resembling a flash grenade pierced through the smoke and landed in the center of the lobby, emitting a powerful burst of light.

Instantly, everyone was temporarily deafened and blinded. At this moment, all white-masked terrorist personnel frantically fired towards where they remembered the bank entrance to be.

'Clang, clang, clang, clang...' A series of metallic impacts. As the smoke cleared, Montagne was seen leading the way with his large shield, all bullets striking its surface, completely failing to cause any effective damage.

Ying, Thermite, and Ash, who entered behind him, seized the opportunity to eliminate the white-masked figures in the lobby one by one.

Meanwhile, the scene instantly switched to the second floor.

Rappelling down, Blackbeard, with his sunglasses and beard, grimly mounted a ballistic shield onto his gun and slowly descended to a boarded-up window on the second floor.

On the rooftop, Pulse looked at the Heartbeat Sensor in his hand; it showed two red dots.

He took out a Remote Charge and placed it above, then retreated to the side.

The next moment, the bomb detonated with a boom, blowing a hole in the roof. In the instant the explosion occurred, Blackbeard burst through the window, his MK17 CQB, equipped with a ballistic shield, swiftly eliminating the two distracted white-masked figures.

Simultaneously, Pulse and Blitz, who jumped down from the rooftop, quickly secured the second floor.

Both the first and second floors were cleared, and the bomb had been defused. Only the hostage room remained.

The two teams converged. Facing the boarded-up wooden door to the hostage room, Ash pulled out her grenade launcher and fired a Breaching Round at the boards.

After a delay of about one second, there was a boom.

The Breaching Round detonated, and smoke filled the air.

At this moment, Blitz, shield in hand, was the first to charge in, pressing the charge button on his shield with a slight force.

Zzzzt... Snap!

A white flash exploded from the shield, illuminating the entire room as if it were daylight.

The sudden blinding flash caused the white-masked figures guarding the hostages to shriek, momentarily blinded, their guns instinctively firing wildly in the direction of the squad.

But countless bullets were all blocked by the shield. However, as bullets struck Blitz's shield.

Visible cracks appeared on the bulletproof glass lens.

However, Blitz remained unfazed, seizing the moment to raise his right hand.

Every bullet fired from the precisely engineered German P12 Pistol accurately pierced the heads of the white-masked terrorist personnel.

Seeing the still-frightened hostages, Ash and Ying stepped forward to untie them.

The next moment, the camera swept across the face of each squad member.

Finally, the screen gradually faded to black.

Ying's voice echoed in the comms channel.

'Assault complete.'

The game title appeared in the center.
