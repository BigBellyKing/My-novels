Chapter 261: Ten Thousand Petitions to Strengthen the Long Sword! (Requesting Subscriptions, Monthly Passes)

Of course, countless players, besides complaining that the "water temperature" in Monster Hunter: World had suddenly risen, also had another matter to grumble about.

This group of players consisted of those wielding the Long Sword.

Long Sword players, one could say, were the largest group of weapon users in Monster Hunter: World.

As for the reason?

Naturally, because it's cool!

However, the situation for Longsword Heroes had always been rather grim.

While the Long Sword's damage output wasn't actually low, and its damage after Red Blade was quite respectable, even if not quite on par with the Great Sword or Charge Blade.

Moreover, the Long Sword was excellent at striking monster hitzones.

Especially the damage from Helm Breaker; as long as it hit a BOSS's hitzone that was susceptible to cutting damage, its output was in no way inferior to the Great Sword's Charged Slash or the Charge Blade's Amped Element Discharge.

Yet, compared to the Charge Blade and Great Sword, the biggest difference was that the Long Sword required the Spirit Gauge State as a prerequisite.

And to achieve the Spirit Gauge State, one had to use that devastating Spirit Roundslash that leaves nothing standing within a five-meter radius.

The Charge Blade and Great Sword users fill their own phials and charge their own attacks; who's like the Long Sword?
