Chapter 275: Trial Version Experience Launched (Requesting Subscriptions, Monthly Passes)

After watching the CG animation released by Chen Xu, both players and designers in the gaming industry were bewildered.

Ordinary players didn't feel much beyond being amazed that an RTS game's CG promotional video could be made so well!

It was significantly better than previous games like Monster Hunter: World and Dark Souls.

A visible improvement!

But for the knowledgeable industry insiders, what was this minute-long video in their eyes?

These were all stacks of red banknotes!

"I'm uncultured, 'holy crap' is all I need to say; this CG is absolutely incredible!"

"Yeah! If Chen Xu hadn't said before that WarCraft is an RTS game, I totally wouldn't have believed it!"

"Are they seriously going to make WarCraft's entire story with CGs of this quality?"

"They couldn't, right? How much would that cost?"

"Didn't Chen Xu say?"

"Holy crap, if that's really the case, does that mean all that massive amount of money was invested purely into CG animation?"

"No way? Nebula Games is a game company, not an animation studio. Is an RTS game really worth such a huge investment?"

"For other companies, I'd say it's impossible, but this is Nebula Games we're talking about! Look at the Nebula Games Platform; could you have imagined someone doing this before?"

"Then that's truly incredible!"

"Now I'm quite curious about the expressions on the designers at Hero Entertainment. Before, Human Wars had a graphical remaster and a major new version update, clearly targeting WarCraft. Now, at the very least, this CG must have utterly surpassed Human Wars."

Many designers and players in the industry discussed the CG promotion released by Chen Xu.

Meanwhile, inside Hero Entertainment, Zhou Qi also looked utterly flabbergasted.

Because the presentation of this CG was simply too exceptional.

This was clearly just brute-forcing it with money!

Could their Hero Entertainment do it?

Absolutely!

After all, this kind of thing relies on money.

But he was certain that a proposal for a graphical remaster could pass.

However, wanting to create CGs of this caliber was basically an impossible task.

Was Chen Xu really pouring seven hundred million into an RTS game?

And most of it was invested in CG production?

Originally, he hadn't believed Nebula Games' boasts about 'redefining RTS' and 'costing 700 million'.

After all, who couldn't boast in promotion?

Like when Human Wars launched previously, he almost claimed it was the strongest RTS game in the galaxy, bar none.

Just like those movies that claimed a production cost of two hundred million, but did they really spend that much?

Not really!

But it had to be said, having money was truly great.

Watching the video on Chen Xu's official Weibo account, Zhou Qi felt a little envious.

Of course, these thoughts were fleeting, and Zhou Qi quickly calmed down.

After all, what good was a CG, no matter how well made?

This was a game, not a movie or an animation.

As an RTS game, it still had to be judged by its gameplay!

So what if your CG was amazing, your graphics were amazing? Were these useful for an RTS game?

Moreover, Chen Xu had never been involved in the RTS genre before. He simply didn't believe that WarCraft could completely blow him away!

Many designers and players shared Zhou Qi's opinion.

Many people were immediately stunned by WarCraft's CG.

However, they soon shifted their attention to the game's content.

In various RTS communities and forums, many players who enjoyed this genre were also discussing it.

"Did you guys follow the news about WarCraft?"

"Of course! That CG was absolutely incredible, I just wonder what the actual game quality will be like."

"I'm sure it won't be bad. After all, Chen Xu made Monster Hunter: World, Minecraft, and Dark Souls before."

"But RTS is different!"

"Yeah, RTS games are different from other genres. Graphics and CGs are just icing on the cake; the core is still gameplay and balance."

"Who cares, isn't there a free trial experience available anyway? Just try it out for ourselves and we'll know, right?"

"That's true."

Along with various discussions among players and industry designers, time slowly arrived at November 11th.

The trial version of WarCraft had already opened its download channels in advance, allowing players to download it early and then enter the game to play as soon as it unlocked.

As for the content of the trial version, it was similar to the previous Monster Hunter: World.

This was because Chen Xu had directly combined the Reign of Chaos and The Frozen Throne from his previous life into a single version.

So, in terms of story, players could fully experience the content of the first chapter, in addition to the prologue.

Which was the story of the disaster that befell the Kingdom of Lordaeron.

However, the online multiplayer battle mode was temporarily in an locked state.

Besides this, during this period, Chen Xu also successively announced some new information about WarCraft.

First was the official launch date, which this time was different compared to Monster Hunter: World.

It was much earlier, with the official version launching on November 15th.

As for the price, the domestic price was 198 Yuan, while the overseas price was 39.9 dollars.

Like before, there was a Steelbook Collector's Edition, priced at 899 Yuan domestically and 159.9 dollars overseas. Compared to the standard digital version, the Collector's Edition included a Blu-ray DVD containing all CGs, a Collector's Edition Frostmourne figure, an art book album, and explanations of relevant races, characters, and worldview background, so it could be said to contain quite a lot of content.

However, simply speaking about the pricing of the digital version, it was relatively low compared to some traditional action, RPG, and FPS blockbusters, but in the RTS genre, it was considered a relatively high standard.

"This price is a bit expensive!"

"Not expensive at all. Look at Chen Xu's previous games; their pricing usually caters to domestic players, and the quality is undeniable. This time, WarCraft's price is considerably higher than similar RTS games, which actually makes me more excited!"

"Exactly, exactly. Before, Chen Xu's games were cheaper than similar types, and their quality was excellent. This time, Nebula Games surprisingly set such a high price, so I think it must be worth the money!"

"One digital version, one collector's edition, no explanation needed!"

"You're awesome, bro, but I'll still download the free trial first to see what the quality is like before I decide."

"Exactly. Anyway, there are still 4 days between the official launch and the trial period. If it's to my taste, I'll just buy it directly then."

Because the strategy of launching a free trial version first was adopted, many players had no concerns whatsoever and eagerly downloaded it, curious to see what the game, with its incredibly impressive CG, was actually like.

(End of chapter)
