Chapter 912: Nebula World

Although for players who often don't dress like proper people in cutscenes, this wave of easter eggs felt like a delicious bowl of Eight Treasure Porridge that had been slightly burnt, leaving a somewhat off taste.

But after a brief moment of awkwardness, many players felt a surge of emotion. After all, no matter what, these were all remnants of their bygone youth.

Besides, this stuff was only visible to them, the players. As long as it wasn't made public, there was no such thing as 'social death'.

After all, who doesn't have some 'dark history'?

As kids, they'd copy song lyrics into notebooks, or write cringeworthy chuuni diaries. After watching wuxia TV shows and movies, they'd grab bamboo sticks with their friends and start the most primitive form of cosplay.

Indecipherable melodramatic phrases in their social media bios, and non-mainstream IDs written in Martian script.

In comparison, not acting like a normal person in a game seemed far less embarrassing when you thought about it.

Countless familiar NPCs emerged from those game scenes, accompanied by the players' thunderous cheers and the melody of the background music.

Chen Xu's figure appeared in the sky.

Facing all the players, he didn't deliver a lengthy speech, only a simple address.

'Thank you to all players for attending Nebula Games' tenth-anniversary celebration. Finally, please experience the joy that Nebula World brings!'

As Chen Xu's words fell, the players remained still, but the NPCs throughout the game erupted in various cheers.

Nebula World brings joy?

Aren't they already in it?

Although the 'Life in Gaming' video was full of emotion, and the gathering of various NPCs had them incredibly excited.

But if it's about joy, this isn't exactly a carnival, is it?

However, the players' confusion didn't last long, because the moment Chen Xu's image vanished from the sky, all players received new notifications.

First, there was the most basic thing: insane discounts.

All games from Nebula Games' first-party and third-party developers were uniformly on discount.

And the discounts were quite substantial.

Secondly, time card-based games like World of Warcraft, as well as games with cosmetics like Rainbow Six Siege and DOTA, all launched special limited edition cosmetics and corresponding, very substantial discounts.

Staring at the uniformly green percentage discounts, many players instantly seemed to lose their minds, rushing to add items to their shopping carts, expressing their inner feelings with three simple words: Buy, buy, buy!

But after checking out, these players suddenly felt that something was amiss.

Damn it, they came to participate in Nebula Games' tenth-anniversary celebration, so why were they damn well spending money?!

Mr. Chen, you're even collecting 'contribution money' from them like this?

Of course, more players were still focused on what Chen Xu had said about Nebula World.

It was not unfamiliar to any of the players present; when Nebula Games first launched, it could be said to have shocked many players.

However, what was then called Nebula World was mostly a way for players to pass the rather long loading times in the early stages, due to hardware limitations.

It was little more than a bonus.

Later, with the development of technology, Nebula World was not abandoned by Nebula Games. Instead, it was continuously updated, gradually replacing the early achievement trophies on the Nebula Games PC platform and becoming new collectible achievement content.

But in terms of its presence, it was relatively weak for most players.

Although it also had various gameplay modes built in, these were mostly for collecting achievement-like content, or just experiencing the game's visuals and simple gameplay.

But now, at this tenth-anniversary celebration, according to Chen Xu, it seemed to have become a new focal point?
