While they were playing Sanguosha, at the neighboring booth, Fall Guys also attracted quite a number of players who sat down at the exhibition's demo stations to try out the game.

Although each match of Fall Guys required gathering 60 players, Chen Xu had specially prepared QR codes allowing players to directly download the game onto their phones. In terms of controls, operating by touching glass screens wasn't quite the same as using a keyboard and mouse.

But in terms of current entertainment value, it was definitely good enough.

And once the official version launched later, it would include a cross-platform feature.

Players could choose whether to enable it for either the PC platform or mobile platforms.

If disabled, players could only match with others on the same platform.

Besides that, Fall Guys was inherently a pick-up-and-leave game.

As soon as a player got eliminated and didn't want to spectate, they could instantly exit and jump straight into another round.

This looping mode ensured that even during the exhibition, every player could start a new match after only a short wait.
