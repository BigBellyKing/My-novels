Compared to sandbox games, open-world games were undoubtedly another monumental game type,

Both shared one core principle: giving players a sense of freedom.

Sandbox games allowed players to create their own worlds, while open-world games involved designers building a world for players to inhabit.

That's why many players often confused sandbox, diorama, and open-world games as the same genre.

To deliver a truly lifelike open-world experience, NPCs were essential.

Thus, after several tests, Chen Xu decided on Monster Hunter, as it aligned perfectly with current feedback.
