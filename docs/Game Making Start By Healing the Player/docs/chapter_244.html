A gentle breeze blew, rustling the leaves and grass.

In a tranquil ancient forest, a Hunter carrying a Great Sword walked through thick vines, advancing deep into the serene woods.

It was an island, where towering trees and tangled vines formed a primeval jungle.

Strange creatures and lush green plants surrounded the area, with hanging vines and dense grass everywhere.

Plants, birds, beasts‚Äîeverything here bore no resemblance to the real-world.

As the Jagras inside the nest rose, the Hunter quietly hid in the tall grass nearby.

Only after all the Jagras dispersed did the Hunter resume his search.

Finally, after navigating the complex forest, the Hunter found his prey.

A colossal creature resembling a Tyrannosaurus Rex.

The camera gave a close-up of the creature, while subtitles on the side revealed its name: Anjanath.

The Hunter donned a Ghillie Mantle resembling green leaves and cautiously approached the Anjanath.

Seizing the moment, the Hunter launched an attack, bringing his Great Sword down hard on the Anjanath's head.

But the damage was minimal, and instead, the massive beast was enraged.

Letting out a thunderous roar, the Anjanath spotted the Hunter and charged toward him with brutal ferocity.

Yet this was exactly what the Hunter had anticipated. He remained completely calm as the beast rushed forward. With two quick rolls backward, he evaded the charge.

The charging Anjanath was suddenly caught and tripped by a Vine Trap, crashing heavily to the ground.

Struggling furiously, the beast kicked and thrashed to free itself, while the Hunter seized the opportunity to unleash relentless damage with his Great Sword.

As the Hunter's sword struck repeatedly, the Anjanath quickly became covered in wounds. But instead of weakening, the injured beast grew even more furious.

Breaking free from the vines, the Anjanath launched relentless attacks at the Hunter, who for a moment struggled to keep up.

Though seemingly at a disadvantage, the Hunter remained as calm as before, dodging the Anjanath's attacks while carefully observing his surroundings.

He pulled out a Flare from his gear and fired it into the sky.

The scene shifted‚Äîthree Wingdrakes appeared in the sky, flying away from the forest.

Hanging beneath them were three Hunters, each gripping a claw, all carrying Long Swords, swiftly approaching.

As the Three Longsword Heroes descended from the sky, they left behind three cool, confident silhouettes.

The camera then returned to the Rathalos' camp, where the Anjanath and Rathalos continued their fierce battle.

Meanwhile, the Hunter had retreated to a safe distance. From somewhere, he pulled out a Folding Stool and a Portable Grill, calmly beginning to prepare some roasting meat.

At that moment, the game's title appeared in the center of the screen.

'Monster Hunter: World'!

Then, accompanied by a furious roar from the Rathalos, a burst of flames erupted from its mouth.

The next moment, a line of text appeared in the center of the screen: Trial Version Available August 1st.
