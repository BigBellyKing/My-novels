Author's Note on Release

Woo... it's officially released today. I don't really know what to say in an author's note, so to make a long story short...

How far a book can go depends on its subscriptions. I hope everyone can support it.

The final update will be released around 6 PM. Because I don't have any drafts saved up (sobbing), I'll update as much as I can, but it won't be less than ten thousand characters.

The cumulative tips total 120,000 points, with 10,000 points counting as one update, for a total of 12 extra updates.

Of course, it's impossible to release them all at once. With a minimum of 2 updates, I'll pay them back gradually.

Finally, thanks to all my readers, and thanks to my editor, Niqiu.

That's it, mwah. I hope everyone can support the legitimate subscriptions.
