Facing the countless players' confusion and speculation, Chen Xu did not directly respond after releasing the first promotional trailer. Instead, he successively released some character concept art and related game settings.

For example, the Tarnished, and Melina, who guides players in increasing their strength, among others.

The release of this content, although without actual game footage, led players to a common realization: this really might be a Souls-like game.

Because based on the revealed settings and art style, the 'flavor' was incredibly strong!

Especially the Redmane led by Starscourage Radahn and the Cleanrot Knights under the Valkyrie, seeing those familiar armors immediately reminded players of Dark Souls.

Amidst all the players' discussions, anticipation, and controversies,

Elden Ring officially launched.

Unlike its previous life, in addition to being VR-adapted, Chen Xu made two very important changes to the game.

In its previous life, one of the main criticisms players had about Elden Ring was not only the numerical balance issues but also 'input reading.'

To give a simple example of 'input reading,' in Sekiro, Genichiro, when a player drinks from their Healing Gourd, would inevitably pull out his great bow to shoot them in the face during his first two phases, and in the third phase, he would invariably use a Thrust Attack that could be Mikiri Countered.

In response, Chen Xu utilized AI to better enable the game's BOSSes to plan their moves.

Of course, it was still a process of memorization.

Secondly, concerning the Ashes of War for summoning spirits, the BOSSes also had a dedicated Aggro analysis system.

"I wonder if Elden Ring is a Souls-like game! If it is, an open-world Souls-like game sounds incredibly interesting!" Song Ren and Zhang Yi chatted in the voice channel while waiting for the game to launch.

"Don't worry, if you encounter a BOSS you can't beat, you can team up with me. Your Sun-Daddy will help you defeat it," Zhang Yi said with a chuckle, wasting no time in taking advantage of Song Ren's words.

"Shut up, you noob! Didn't you always ask me to team up to get souls when you couldn't beat a BOSS before? Forgotten already?" Song Ren retorted disdainfully.

"Proof! A society governed by law requires proof, otherwise, you're slandering me!" Zhang Yi shamelessly argued.

"Just keep talking nonsense! I'm not blabbering with you, I'm going to check out the game first!" Song Ren snorted, then, seeing that it was time, quickly entered the game.

As the opening CG concluded, Song Ren was 80% certain.

This CG style!

It was definitely that distinctive flavor!

From the storyline, isn't the Tarnished just an Undead?

Since he was almost 100% sure this was a Souls-like game,

As an experienced veteran Undead, he understood perfectly!

Regarding the starting Keepsake, he didn't understand many of the items, so after some thought, he chose the versatile [Golden Seed]; from the description, it could give him an extra Blood Vial.

Clearly, the Blood Vials in Elden Ring corresponded to Dark Souls and Sekiro, not like in Bloodborne.

With his class confirmed, after darkness, Song Ren appeared in a room. From a nearby corpse, he picked up a Tarnished's Old Paper. Having played Souls-like games, he was familiar with it; it was an item for leaving in-game messages.

But what surprised him the most was that the game's controls were not an entirely new combat system like Sekiro: Shadows Die Twice.

Instead, it was like Dark Souls, the only difference being that the Undead now had knees and could jump.

Ignoring the new player tutorial messages on the ground, Song Ren practiced in the dark room and quickly figured out the basic action commands, as he already had experience.

He then opened the equipment interface and confirmed that he was not overweight.

"So, this is an open-world Dark Souls! Isn't that invincible?"

"As an experienced Firelinker, this journey to become king should be smooth sailing, right? I'm destined to be the Elden Lord!"
